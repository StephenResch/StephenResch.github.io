About Get To School

Get To School is an indie game about the perilous journey to priso- I mean school. You will have to raid dungeons, kill a
god or two, steal crates of fish while getting liquified by a middle-aged snob who has grown tired of living, and more!
This game was entirely made with RPG Maker, so don't expect too much. Future updates for the game will include story
progression, secret content, but most importantly, random #@!$ that I throw in because I'm bored.
